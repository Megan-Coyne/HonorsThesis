The Detroit Economic Conditions Index (ECI) tracks economic growth in the Detroit-Warren-Dearborn area from February 1990 to December 2019. It combines 12 key economic indicators, calibrated to Gross Metropolitan Product growth. This EDA examines trends and patterns in the ECI to understand Detroit’s economic conditions. Data obtained from the Federal Reserve Bank of St. Louis via FRED®.
