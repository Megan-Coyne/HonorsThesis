Research and Modeling

In this section, I explore the application of Extreme Value Theory (EVT) to both corporate and municipal financial data. By employing the Generalized Extreme Value (GEV) distribution, I model the behavior of rare but significant financial events, such as extreme declines in stock returns or municipal revenue. This approach allows for a rigorous assessment of tail risks, capturing the probability of extreme outcomes beyond typical financial fluctuations.
